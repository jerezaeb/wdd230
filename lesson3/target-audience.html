Target Audience

Our target audience are business owners in the area who are seeking to expand their business through building relationship with potential clients and partnership with business owners in the area. We are also after the investors who are interested in helping business owners expand their business.


Personas

Danny is a 35-year-old small business owner who runs a manpower services in the local community. Danny uses the internet to hire employees and look for clients. He is interested in networking opportunities and attending local events to promote their manpower services business and connect with other entrepreneurs in the community. Danny is also looking for resources and support to help them navigate the challenges of running a small business in today's economic climate.


Patty is a 45-year-old Investor in the area. Patty is interested in connecting with other professionals in the region to explore new business opportunities and partnership in the local community

Scenarios

Business owners who would like to attend the networking event. Business owners can sign up to be the member of the organization. They can search for the networking events, register online and will be provided confirmation and instruction about the upcoming event.

Business Development resources provides continued improvement of knowledge and skills upgrade to business owners.
